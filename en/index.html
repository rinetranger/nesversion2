This is Japan's first large-scale natural adventure theme park.
Spanning approximately 2.3 million m², the vast grounds feature over 40 types of activities that can be enjoyed with a 1-Day Pass, including accommodation facilities and a natural hot spring surrounded by nature, totaling over 60 facilities.
